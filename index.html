Takolucky

Welcome to Takolucky, where we transport you to the bustling streets of Osaka with every bite of our authentic Japanese street food favorite. We specialize in bite-sized, golden-brown octopus balls that deliver a delightful textural contrast: a crispy exterior shell enveloping a hot, soft, and dashi-rich center, generously filled with tender pieces of real octopus, green onions, and pickled ginger. Each order is generously slathered in our signature savory sauce and Japanese mayonnaise, finished with a flourish of dancing bonito flakes and aromatic aonori (seaweed powder), creating a rich, umami-packed flavor experience that is perfect as a satisfying snack, appetizer, or a fun, shareable meal for friends and family.
